Congressional
Gold Medal Dinner

Friday, June 12, 2015

A special fundraising dinner, organized to support the Congressional Gold Medal Digital Exhibition which highlights the stories of the World War II Nisei soldiers, will be held on Friday, June 12, at the Fairmont Hotel in San Jose. The event will feature a special thank you to former U.S. Secretary of Veterans Affairs and 34th U.S Army Chief of Staff,  Eric Shinseki.Special guests include Secretary and Mrs. Norman Mineta and Kristi Yamaguchi, Olympic Gold Medalist. All veterans will be recognized.
 

To purchase tickets or to dontate, please visit www.nationalveteransnetwork.com/dinner

For information about the dinner, please contact Reiko Iwanaga at riwanaga@ecopiafarms.com.

For more information about the exhibition, visit www.nationalveteransnetwork.com.
