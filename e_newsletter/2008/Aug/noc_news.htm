NOC General Membership Meeting
   


9066 to 9/11:  America's Concentration Camps,
Then ...and Now?
 Film Screening

2-4 pm, Sunday, September 21, 2008

Yu-Ai Kai Building
588 North 4th Street,
San Jose, CA
 

Come and find out what NOC and the Day of Remembrance event is all about.
 
  At this general meeting, we will discuss ideas for the 2009 Day of Remembrance event. We will also screen the documentary "9066 to 9/11: America's Concentration Camps, Then...and Now." This short film draws parallels between the treatment of Japanese Americans during World War II with the treatment of Arab Americans and Muslims in this country after 9/11. Contact info@sjnoc.org for more info.
