“In God’s House”

August 12, 2007

Wesley United Methodist Church

 

NOC and Wesley UMC cosponsored a historic film screening of the ground breaking film, “In God’s House, Asian American Lesbian & Gay Families in the Church” by Lina Hoshino.

 

The film was followed with a compelling panel discussion with Dr. Elizabeth Leung, Rev. Nabuaki Hanaoka, Rev Jeffrey Kuan, Oneida Chi and Rev. Lloyd Wake.

 

NOC is a strong supporter of LGBT rights and is pledged to promote tolerance and acceptance of all people. We hope that this event is a first step in that direction.
