Direct bus service from San Jose to the "End the War" 
rally is available. The bus will leave San Jose at 9am 
and will return at around 4:30-5pm.
  
Tickets are now available at the San Jose Peace Center 
(www.sanjosepeace.org) with a sliding scale of $10-$20, 
meaning that if you can afford a $20 ticket, please 
purchase your tickets at that price so that we can make 
tickets available at $5 or less for students and 
other low-income individuals.  
People should be at the primary pickup location at the 
County Building parking lot at First and Hedding at 9 a.m. 
or at the secondary pickup location at De Anza College on 
Stevens Creek next to the parking garage at 10 a.m. 
Call the San Jose Peace Center (408) 297-2299 or visit 
the Center to reserve your seat on the bus.
Taking the bus is convenient, it costs less than the train 
+ Muni, and you'll meet many new friends.
